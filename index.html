Scope & limitations (click)

These sandboxes are built as intuition builders and presentation companions. They are not used as empirical identification strategies.

  • Risk-neutral assumptions only apply where explicitly stated (e.g., Gaussian/Black–Scholes baseline).
  • Some models are deliberately stylized / ad hoc to visualize mechanisms (not market-calibrated).
  • Not for trading or production risk management.
